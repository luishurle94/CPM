The time has come to choose where to seat. I'm nervous!. Choosing a good seating is very important: If you seat too close to the screen you'll end with pain in on your neck for looking up continuously, and if you are too far, you'll need a telescope.
Well, maybe I'm exagerating thins a bit, but let's go anyway:

Wait, take me back!

Where do you fancy seating?

Press over the seats you want for you. For the record, the upper row of seats is located closer to the screen.

Wait! I need more than one ticket

No problem, I got you! Just press over all the seats you want, and the total number of tickets you are going to buy will appear indicated in the lower part of the screen. Leave space for other people, though!

Better not seat over someone else!

Before choosing a seat, make sure it's not chosen already:

  • Crossed gray seat: This seat has been chosen by someone else, and you can't choose it
  • White seat: This seat is free, and you may choose it
  • Checked blue seat: You have chosen this seat for you. Press again to remove selection

Hey! I can't select any seats!

If the room looks like this, all seats are occupied. You can't buy tickets for that combination of movie, date and time. Try with different movie, date or time.
