Taking advantage until the last moment

If you are lucky, your purchase may be cheaper. On this screen you can see all available discounts. Check that you meet all requirements to choose a discount before selecting it.

Yeah... let's get on with it

To select and apply a discount, press over it. On the lower part of the screen you'll see how the final price changes in real time. When you are done, press 'Continue'
